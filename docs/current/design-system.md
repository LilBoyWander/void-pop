# Design System

Void Pop uses a dark, restrained sci-fi arcade presentation.

## Direction

- Near-black and navy foundations with subtle nebula/star depth
- Cyan as the primary player/interface signal
- Magenta/red for hostile or dangerous states
- Bright size-dependent sphere colors with controlled glow
- Compact uppercase HUD typography and technical framing
- Effects support readability; particles, bloom, and shake remain restrained

## Interaction

Keyboard is primary: A/D or arrows move, Space starts/fires/restarts, Escape
pauses, and M toggles audio. Touch layouts provide a virtual movement stick and
fire control. Fullscreen is user initiated. Controls and overlays require clear
labels and accessible names.

## Acceptance

Automated checks may confirm DOM wiring, overflow, and runtime stability, but the
human approves gameplay readability, visual hierarchy, audio, animation, and
responsive feel.
